3

Outreach Campaign

Completed

Use this low-pressure, high-value message sequence. The goal: start a conversation, not a sales pitch.

Message 1: The Opener

Hey [Name], I'm putting together some case studies for [Your ICP] and your name popped up. I was wondering if you know anyone in that space who struggles with [Problem from your Card]? Any pointers would be a huge help.

Message 2: The Pivot

Awesome, thanks! Essentially, I solve that with what I call a "[Automation Card Title]." It's a system that automatically [High-level solution]. Does that concept make sense?

Message 3: The Value Offer

Glad it resonates. I put together a one-page summary of how it worked for a client (they got back ~5 hours a week). No strings attached, but would that be helpful for you to see?

Message 4: The Ask

Here you go: [Link to Card]. If you feel a similar approach could be useful, I'd be open to a quick 15-min chat next week to explore it. Let me know what you think!
4

Follow-up & Track

Completed

Persistence is key. Not everyone will reply to your first message. Stay organized and celebrate the conversations.

Follow up on Message 1 after 5-7 business days.
Keep your tracking sheet updated religiously.
Celebrate conversations started, not just deals closed.
